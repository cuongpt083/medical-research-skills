# Final Medical Research Report Contract

Mandatory sections:

1. **Executive answer** — direct answer proportional to evidence.
2. **Question interpretation** — structured clinical/research question.
3. **Evidence summary** — grouped by hierarchy and relevance.
4. **Key claims** — every material claim maps to verified evidence.
5. **Conflicting evidence** — disagreements are explicit.
6. **Uncertainty** — unknown, indirect, imprecise, outdated or preliminary evidence.
7. **Clinical caveats** — distinguish population-level evidence from patient-specific decisions.
8. **Research method** — mode, source classes, dates, stopping rule, degraded lanes.
9. **References** — authoritative/primary sources preferred for material claims.
10. **Verification & safety** — citation status, preprint/regulatory/guideline caveats, safety disposition.

Forbidden:
- hiding unresolved conflicts;
- topic-related but non-supportive citations;
- converting relative effects to absolute effects without data;
- equating publication with approval;
- presenting preprints as established guidance;
- silently personalizing treatment recommendations.
